About Event

Avinyaa is a national level technical symposium organized by the department of Artificial Intelligence And Machine Learning of R.M.D. Engineering College . Avinyaa signifies INNOVATION. Innovation is any change big or small that makes a difference. Avinyaa enables the student to step out of their comfort zone to explore their talents.This symposium acts as a Symbiotic relationship between participants and organising team. Avinyaa is a one day event where enthralling technical and non technical events are conducted.

No Plagiarism

Rules and Regulations

  • 1.Topics for paper presentation must be based only on Artificial Intelligence or Machine learning.
  • 2. Paper format must be based on the IEEE standard.
  • 3.Paper and abstract must be submitted THREE days prior to the event.
  • 4.The paper must not be published before the event
  • 5.Palagiarism level must be below 20%.

ANALYTICON

Rules and Regulations

  • 1. Participants are required to bring their own laptops.
  • 2. The use of pre trained models from scikit learn is restricted
  • 3. Data processing modules from scikit learning can be used
  • 4. Participants can make use of Tensorflow, PyTorch frameworks.
  • 5. The dataset will be provided in the form of CSV data.
  • 6. Performance of the model will be evaluated based on Confusion matrix and F1 accuracy score.
